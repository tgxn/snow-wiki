 This plugin has been retired and is no longer available for new activations (since Eureka and unavailable for activation in Dublin).

This page applies only to existing users of the Sales Force Automation plugin.

1 Overview

In the Sales Force Automation application, contracts manage sales records for products or services. Contracts may have a limited time frame, track specific post-sale details, and identify renewal opportunities. Administrators and users with the sales_operation role can create and manage contracts. Sales users can view contract information.

Contracts. Manage contracts for won opportunities.


2 Creating Contracts

To create a sales contract for a won opportunity:

  1. Open the opportunity.
  2. Click the Won related link, if necessary.
  3. Click the Create Contract related link. The contract is linked to the account and opportunity.
  4. Enter the contract details and save the record.
Contract form


3 Managing Contracts

Contracts
To manage sales contracts:
  • To view contracts associated with various records, navigate to Sales > Sales Operations > Contracts.
  • To view contracts associated with a specific account, navigate to the account and view the Contracts related list.
