1 New Look

The new Wiki has a cleaner look:

In particular, the new wiki adds a ServiceNow toolbar to easily navigate to other ServiceNow sites:

2 New Search

In the upper-right hand corner, there is now a Custom Google Search that searches both the main Wiki and the community:

You can get it for your browser as well. For more information, see Custom Google Search.

3 Tree Menus

The new Tree Menu interface allows easy navigation through hierarchical lists.

This is now used on the main page, as a Table of Contents:

In the navigational side-bar:

And in topic boxes:

3.1 Using the Tree Menu

To collapse or expand a level in the Tree Menu, click the + or - box to the left of the topic:

4 Breadcrumb Navigation

Topics now display a breadcrumb trail under the article title, making it easy to see where in the wiki the current article is:

5 Feedback Tools

The upgraded wiki now has two methods of providing feedback on article:

  • Threaded Discussion Pages
  • Ratings

To use these tools, you must be logged in.

5.1 Threaded Discussion Pages

To comment on a Wiki Article, log in and click the Discussion link on the page:

The New, Edit, and Reply tabs at the right of every thread can be used to create new threads, or edit or reply to existing ones.

5.2 Rating Pages

At the bottom of each page, there is also now a new feedback box:

When feedback is submitted, a confirmation will appear that displays the ratings of the page:
