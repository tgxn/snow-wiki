1 Overview

With the ideation module, business users can create and submit ideas. The demand manager evaluates the submitted ideas and promotes accepted ideas to demands. This module also tracks the progress of an idea in one location as it moves through the different stages of the life cycle, from idea to demand to development and actualization. Ideation is integrated with the Demand application and is available starting with the Fuji release.

Business users create and submit ideas through the Service Catalog and then collaborate on these ideas using Live Feed. Each idea is created with a unique idea number, which is displayed at the top of the Self Service homepage immediately after submission. Clicking this link opens the Idea form.

After an idea is submitted, the demand manager can review the idea, make any necessary modifications, and then choose to accept or defer the idea. If accepted, the idea is promoted to a strategic or organizational demand. After an idea is promoted to a demand, the idea becomes read-only.

2 Submitting Ideas

All users can submit ideas through the Service Catalog. Demand users can submit ideas through the Self-Service menu.

  1. Navigate to one of the following:
    • For all users: Self-Service > Service Catalog. Click Can We Help You? and then click Submit Idea.
    • For demand users: Self-Service > Ideas > Create New.
  2. Type a brief description of the idea in the Title field.
  3. Describe a business justification that supports the idea in the Description field.
  4. Click Submit.
    The system creates a new idea, assigns a unique number, and adds it to the Ideas list. The new idea is created in the Submitted state.
Note: After an idea is submitted, it cannot be modified.


Ideas List

3 Viewing Ideas

Users can view their own submitted ideas in the Service Catalog by navigating to Self-Service > Ideas > My Ideas. Users can view all submitted ideas by navigating to Self-Service > Ideas > All. Ideas are displayed on the Ideas list, which includes the following four columns:

  • Idea: initially displays both the title of an idea and the idea number. Idea is a composite field, with the idea number providing a link to the Idea form.
  • Stage: displays the current stage of an idea as it moves through the life cycle. Pointing to this field opens a pop-up window that shows the progression from idea to demand to actualization. For more information, see Stage Fields.
  • Submitted by: displays the name of the user who submitted the idea.
  • Demand: displays the demand name and number after an idea has been accepted and promoted to a demand. Clicking the demand number in this composite field opens the Demand form.

3.1 Stage Fields

The Stage field on the Ideas list displays the current state of an idea as it moves through the demand life cycle, from an idea to a demand and then to the resulting project, enhancement, change, or defect. The result of an idea depends on the initial settings in the Category and Type fields on the Demand form. The Stage field also appears on the Demands list. The Stage field is a display only field and cannot be searched or sorted.

A new idea is created in the Submitted state. An accepted idea is updated to the Accepted state and a deferred idea is updated to the Skipped state. When a demand is closed, the associated idea is marked as Complete.

Pointing to the Stage field displays a pop-up window that shows the progression of the idea along with the value of the State field for each step in the progression:

  • Idea: If an idea is being evaluated, the pop-up window displays the current state of the idea.
  • Demand: if an idea is promoted to a demand, the pop-up window displays the current state of the demand as well as the idea.
  • Project, Enhancement, Change, or Defect: if a demand is accepted, the pop-up window displays the current state of the resulting project, enhancement, change, or defect in addition to the demand and idea.
Idea Stage Summary pop-up window

4 Collaborating On Ideas

Users can use the activity stream, Live Feed, or collaboration tools to discuss ideas and exchange information.

  • On the Ideas list header, click the list activity stream icon to display the activity stream for the records currently displayed in the list.
  • On the Idea form header, click the show live feed icon to display the live feed information specific to this record.
  • On the Idea form header, click the collaboration icon () to use the collaboration tool.

5 Evaluating Ideas

The demand manager can evaluate an idea and then decide whether to accept the idea, defer it, or delete it from the Ideas list.

  1. Navigate to Self-Service > Ideas > All.
  2. Select an idea and click the idea number.
  3. Click one of the following buttons:
    • Update: saves any changes made to the idea form.
    • Accept: changes the idea state to Accepted and creates the associated demand record.
    • Defer: defers the idea and changes the idea state to Skipped.
    • Delete: removes the idea from the Ideas list.

The demand manager can also select multiple ideas on the Ideas list and then select Accept, Defer, or Delete from the Actions choice list.

6 Integration with Project Portfolio Suite

The Ideas module is a component of Demand Management. It can be activated on its own or can be activated as part of the Project Portfolio Suite (PPS), starting with the Fuji release.

When the Ideas module is activated as part of the Project Portfolio Suite, the demand manager role is added to the project manager role, enabling project managers to also approve ideas. For more information, see User Roles.
