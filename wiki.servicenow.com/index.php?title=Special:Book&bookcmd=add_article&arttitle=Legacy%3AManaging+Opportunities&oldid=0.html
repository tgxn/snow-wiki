This page applies only to existing users of the Sales Force Automation plugin.

1 Overview

In the Sales Force Automation application, opportunities represent potential sales of products or services.

Opportunities. Track potential sales as opportunities.


2 Creating Sales Opportunities

To create a sales opportunity:

  1. Open the customer account.
  2. In the Opportunities related list, click New.
  3. Enter basic opportunity details and save the record.
    • Account - company for which the sales proposal is intended
    • Owner - sales representative managing the opportunity
    • Probability - estimate (percent) of the likelihood of closing the deal, possibly within a given time period
    • Stage - phase in the sales process (discovery, qualification, validation, proposal, negotiation, closing)
    • Type - sales category for use in reports (new business, up-sell, cross-sell)
  4. In the Line Items related list, add products to the sales proposal. The sum of line item prices populates the opportunity total value.
    • Product - select from the product list. Base price and Total price are populated automatically.
    • Quantity, Discount - enter values, if applicable. Total price is calculated automatically.
  5. In the Opportunity contacts related list, select a subset of the account contacts that are the primary contacts for the opportunity.
Line Item


3 Managing Sales Opportunities

View open opportunities
To manage open sales opportunities:
  • To view opportunities associated with various records, navigate to Sales > Opportunities (Open) (all open opportunities) or Sales > My Opportunities (assigned to current user).
  • To view opportunities associated with a specific account, navigate to the account and view the Opportunities related list.
  • Consider using the probability field in filters or reports to focus sales efforts and improve the accuracy of forecasts.
  • To track actions related to pursuing an opportunity, add activities.
  • Adjust the Stage and Probability as the opportunity progresses through the sales process.
  • To close an opportunity, use the Won or Lost Related link. Users with sales_operation access can now create a contract for won opportunities.
