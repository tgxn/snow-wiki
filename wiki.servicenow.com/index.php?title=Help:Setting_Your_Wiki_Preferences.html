Enabling Auto-Numbered Headings

Logged in users can configure their user preferences to display automatically numbered headings for wiki articles.

Page with auto-number headings enabled

To configure auto-numbered headings:

  1. Navigate to My preferences > Misc.
  2. Select the Auto-number headings check box.
  3. Click Save.
