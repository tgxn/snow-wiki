1 Overview

The Best Practice - ITIL KPI Reports module provides a series of reports that track the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) of Incident Management and Problem Management, as recommended by ITIL.

1.1 Activating the Plugin

The Best Practice - ITIL KPI Reports (com.snc.bestpractice.itil_kpi) plugin is active by default and creates all of the KPI reports.

2 Reports

To see the ITIL KPI reports, navigate to Reports > View / Run and filter the list on KPI.

Report Name Type Description
KPI - Average Incident Resolution Time by Category Pivot Table Lists the average resolution time and category of incidents closed in the last 12 months.
KPI - Incidents Assigned More than Once by Category Trend Chart Lists the number and category of incidents that have been reassigned to another assignment group one or more times in the last 12 months.
KPI - Number of Incidents by Category Trend Chart Lists the number and category of incidents created in the last 12 months.
KPI - Number of Incidents per Month by Category Line Chart Lists the number of incidents created per month in the last 12 months.
KPI - Number of Repeated Incidents Trend Chart Lists the number and close codes of incidents closed per month in the last 12 months.
KPI - Remotely Resolved Incidents Trend Chart Lists the number and remote close code of incidents closed per month in the last 12 months.
KPI - First Time Resolution Rate by Category Pivot Table Lists the number and category of incidents with a First Call Resolution definition in the last 12 months.
KPI - Incident Resolution within SLA by Category Bar Chart Lists the number and category of incidents closed in the Achieved or Breached stage in the last 12 months.
KPI - Average Work Effort for Resolving Incidents by Category Pivot Table Lists the average time worked and category of incidents closed in the last 12 months.
KPI - Number of Incidents per Problem List Lists all active problem records with one or more related incidents.
KPI - Number of Problems by State and CI Pivot Table Lists the number, state, and affected CI of problems created in the last 12 months.
KPI - Average Problem Resolution Time Trend Chart Lists the average resolution time and category of problems closed in the last 12 months.
