1 Overview

This article details the installed components, system dependencies, and impact of the Sales Force Automation Plugin.

2 Applications & Features

Sales application
 The Sales application is added to the navigation pane along with modules for managing sales and marketing records:
  • Leads
  • Accounts
  • Contacts
  • Activities
  • Opportunities
  • Products
  • Contracts

The following mobile modules are also included:

  • Customer (Accounts)
  • My Accounts
  • My Contacts
  • My Opportunities
  • Products

A Sales perspective for the navigation menu is included.

The Sales Performance homepage provides standard sales report gauges.

Access to most sales features requires the sales role. For detailed access and security information, see User Roles and Security Rules.

2.1 Demo Data

Demo data is available with this plugin.


3 Database Table Structure

3.1 Existing Tables

The following tables are modified:

Display Name (Table Name) Modification
Company (core_company)
  • Add field to identify sales partners
  • Add reference field to relate company to sales account, if applicable
User (sys_user)
 Add reference fields to relate user to sales contact and account, if applicable

3.2 New Tables

The following tables are added:

Display Name (Table Name) Description
Key Contact (sales_account_key_contact)
 Identifies account contacts as key roles within the account (e.g., business contact, technical lead). Roles are defined in the sales_contact_role table.
Account (sales_account)
 Similar to a company record, stores all company related information associated with the sales cycle
Activity (sales_activity)
 Sales related activity tasks used with sales accounts, opportunities, and contacts. Extends the task table.
Company Product Component (sales_company_component)
 Provides a way to track product components used by a company if accounts are migrated to companies (e.g., MSPs may store accounts as companies).
Competes with Component (sales_competitor_prod_comp_m2m)
 Relationship table that associates competitor products with sales_product_component
Competes with Product (sales_competitor_prod_product_m2m)
 Relationship table that associates competitor products with sales_product
Competitor Product (sales_competitor_product)
 Competitor's product that can be associated to one or more sales_products or sales_product_components
Contact role (sales_contact_role)
 Available roles that a contact can have within the account
Contact (sales_contact)
 A person-type record associated with a sales account, typically generated from a sales lead
Contract (sales_contract)
 Record to track the life of the sales agreement, typically generated once an opportunity is won
Industry (sales_industry)
 A collection of business industries, referenced by sales accounts and leads
Sales Assignment Rule (sales_lead_assignment_rule)
 Assignment rules for auto-assigning a lead to a sales rep based on some condition
Lead Source (sales_lead_source)
 Allows sales leads to be tracked against a source from which the lead originated
Lead (sales_lead)
 The starting point of the sales lifecycle, represents a person and company information that has the potential to be useful to the sales department
Location (sales_location)
 A location reference record used for accounts and contacts as an option to using the address fields within those records
Opportunity Contacts (sales_opportunity_contact_m2m)
 Identifies a subset of the accounts contacts that are the contacts associated with an opportunity
Line Items (sales_opportunity_item)
 A sales opportunity product line item
Opportunity (sales_opportunity)
 Primary record for a sales proposal, used with related list of opportunity line items
Product Components (sales_product_component_m2m)
 Relationship table that associates products with product components
Product Component (sales_product_component)
 Provides child components within a product if implementation uses sales_product records as bundles
Product Discount (sales_product_discount)
 Not yet implemented.
Product (sales_product)
 A product that is available for sale, used with opportunity line items

4 User Roles

The following user roles are added:

Role Description
sales Access to the sales application and general sales functions.
inside_sales No out-of-box access rights.
sales_operations Inherits the sales role and add access to advanced sales functions, including:
  • Creating and managing contracts
  • Managing product and competitor product information
  • Managing Lead Assignment Rules
  • Managing Lead Sources

Required to access the following modules:

  • Key Contact Roles
  • Lead Assignment Rules
 Note: If you are using high security settings in default deny mode, you must add an ACL to the sales_lead_assignment table to allow users with the sales_operation role to manage lead assignment rules.


sales_director Inherits the sales role.
sales_partner Inherits the sales role. Access to view contacts and manage leads.
marketing When granted with the sales role, access to manage lead sources. Otherwise, no out-of-box access rights.

5 Security Rules (ACL)

The following security rules (ACL) are included:

Name Operation Description
sales_account RWC sales role required to read/write/create sales account records
sales_account_key_contact WCD sales role required to write/create/delete sales account key contact records
sales_activity RW sales role required to read/write sales activity records
sales_activity.assigned_to, .description, .short_description, .state W sales role required to write to assigned to, description, short description, and state fields on activity records
sales_competitor_product R sales role required to read competitor product records
sales_contact WC sales role required to write/create sales contact records
sales_contact R sales or sales_partner role required to read sales contact records
sales_contract R sales role required to read sales contract records
sales_contract W sales_operations role required to write sales contract records
sales_lead RC sales or sales_partner role required to read/create sales lead records
sales_lead W sales_operations role or lead assigned to current user required to write sales lead records
sales_lead_source R sales or marketing role required to read sales lead source records
sales_lead_source WC sales, sales_operation, or marketing role required to write/create sales lead source records
sales_location.full_name W nobody can write to the full name field on sales location records
sales_opportunity RWC sales role required to read/write/create sales opportunity records
sales_product R sales role required to read sales product records
sales_product W sales_operations role required to write sales product records
sales_product_component RW sales role required to read/write sales product component records
sales_product_component_m2m RWD sales role required to read/write/delete the sales product component relationship table

6 Business Rules

The following business rules are added:

Name (Table Name) Description
Set Customer Date (sales_account) sets customer since date field when type changes to customer
Set account from contact or opp (sales_activity) sets the account field if available from the contact or opportunity
Link lead to contact by email (sales_lead) when lead is inserted or updated, checks for a contact with a matching email address. If one exists, links the two by setting the account and contact fields.
Assign Sales lead (sales_lead) processes lead assignment rules
Set Probability Percent (sales_opportunity) sets the probability percent field from the probability value, used to display percent gauge on list view
Update Opportunity Value (sales_opportunity_item) sums opportunity line items and enters the total in opportunity
Update Total Price (sales_opportunity_item) updates item total price when base price, quantity, or discount changes

7 Client Scripts

The following client scripts are added:

Name (Table Name) Description
Set Account from Contact (sales_activity) sets account if value is available from contact
Set Account from Opportunity (sales_activity) sets account if value is available from opportunity
Update Total Price Common (sales_opportunity_item) common function used by the next 3 scripts
Update Total on Base Change (sales_opportunity_item) updates item total price when base price changes
Update Total on Quantity Change (sales_opportunity_item) updates item total price when quantity changes
Update Total on Discount Change (sales_opportunity_item) updates item total price when discount changes
Load base price from product (sales_opportunity_item) when changing product, sets the base price from the product record

8 Dependencies

  • The Competitor Products module accesses the Company table for competitor company information.
  • The widget at the top of the opportunity form requires the Process Flow Formatter plugin (installed automatically with the plugin).

9 Impact

Activating Sales Force Automation adds fields to existing tables. The Process Flow Formatter plugin is installed automatically with Sales Force Automation. You may choose to install demo data with the plugin.
