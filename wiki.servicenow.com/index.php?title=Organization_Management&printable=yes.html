1 Overview

The Organization Management application provides an easy way to perform such asset management tasks as updating users and adding vendors.



All of the modules are also accessible from other applications.

Option Description Also Accessible from For more information
Users Individual system users, often assigned to groups. User Administration Creating Users and Associating to a Group
Cost Centers An entity used to associate IT costs and users consuming IT services. Financial Management Managing Cost Centers
Departments A method of categorizing users, groups, and assets. User Administration Adding a New Department
Companies Companies and organizations your organization does business with. User Administration Adding a New Company
Manufacturers A company that builds assets your organization purchases. User Administration (Companies list filtered for Manufacturers) Vendor and Manufacturer
Vendors A company that sells assets or services your organization purchases. User Administration (Companies list filtered for Vendors) Vendor and Manufacturer
Locations Physical locations identified by such features as city, country, and latitude/longitude. User Administration Defining Locations
Location Map Location determined by using a business rule that queries Google Maps to fill in the latitude and longitude fields. User Administration Map Location

2 Organization Management Views

2.1 Vendor and Manufacturer

To create a new vendor or manufacturer:

  1. Navigate to one of the following:
    • Organization Management > Vendors
    • Organization Management > Manufacturers
    • Organization Management > Companies (Switch to the Default view.)
  2. Click New in the record list.
  3. Complete the form and select either Manufacturer or Vendor.
  4. Click Submit.


2.2 Locations

The Locations module is the Asset view of the Location table [cmn_location] and displays unique information. In addition to the physical specifics for the location, the Asset view includes the Stock room designation and a list of configuration items (CI) in stock at that location.
