1 Overview

Watch lists (glide_list field type) allow multiple users to subscribe to notifications of a task (see Using Watch Lists). Administrators can configure options for watch list fields.

2 Configuring Email Notifications for Watch Lists

To send email updates to users in the Watch list, select Watch list from the Users/groups in fields portion of the email notification.

Note: To receive these notifications, users must have an email address defined in their user record or enter an email address into the watch list email field.


Adding the Watch list to an email notification

3 Configuring Multiple Watch Lists

An advanced configuration using watch lists might involve placing two watch lists on a form, one for the general comments on a ticket and another for work notes or non-public comments. By configuring separate email notifications, separate users on each watch list can be notified about different information.

4 Troubleshooting Duplicate Email Notifications

If users on a watch lists are getting more than one email for each update to an incident, it may be because the people involved in the incident are replying to an email notification in which other user's names appear in the To: or CC: field. Recipients may be receiving email through their email system (Outlook, Groupwise, etc.) and through ServiceNow. To stop this duplication, remove the names of other users from the email or the Watch List.

5 Hiding Email Addresses in a Watch List

You can remove the email address text entry element from a watch list by modifying the dictionary and adding the dictionary attribute no_email=true. You can add new users to the watch list from the reference field lookup.
