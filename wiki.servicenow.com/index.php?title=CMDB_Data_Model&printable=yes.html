The Configuration Management Database (CMDB) is a series of tables containing all the assets and business services controlled by a company and their configurations. Configuration items such as computers and other devices on the network, software contracts and licenses, and business services are represented.

The following figure represents the first few levels of the CMDB tables. These tables represent the class or type of the configuration items.
