1 Overview

Reports can focus sales efforts and improve the accuracy of forecasts. Several standard reports are available in the Sales Force Automation application. Users can also create custom reports to view sales data.

2 Standard Reports

The following standard reports are included with the Sales Force Automation plugin:

Title Table Type Field Sample Image
Lead Conversion sales_lead Bar chart assigned_to
Active Leads By Rep sales_lead Bar chart assigned_to
Lead Status sales_lead Pie chart lead_status
Active Pipeline sales_opportunity Horizontal bar probability
Top Closers sales_opportunity Bar chart owner
Quarterly Win Trend sales_opportunity Trend chart type
Quarterly Win/Loss Ratio sales_opportunity Trend chart stage
Top Products 12 months sales_opportunity_item Bar chart product


3 Sales Performance Dashboard

The Sales Performance dashboard is the Sales homepage. Users and administrators can customize the dashboard. All standard sales reports are available to add as gauges.

To open the dashboard, navigate to Sales > Sales Performance.
