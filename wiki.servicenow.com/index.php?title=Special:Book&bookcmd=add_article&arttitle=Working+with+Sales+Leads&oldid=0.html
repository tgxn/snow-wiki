1 Overview

The sales cycle starts with a lead, a person at a prospective customer company. A lead is either validated as a sales contact, then converted to an account and contact, or it is closed. Leads may be assigned automatically or manually to a sales representative for validation.

A lead contains contact information and a basic company profile. It may be created manually or imported from an external source.

Lead life cycle. A valid lead is converted into new contact and account records.


2 Working with Sales Leads

Open lead
To work with a lead:
  1. Navigate to Sales > Leads (all leads) or Sales > My Leads (assigned to current user).
  2. Click New or open the lead to edit.
  3. Enter the lead details and save the record.
    • Lead status
      • If the lead needs to be validated, select New.
      • If the lead has no value, select Dead. The lead no longer appears in the My Leads list for the assigned representative.
      • If the lead is validated as a potential sales contact, select Active. The Convert link appears on the form.
    • Lead source - select a lead source, if applicable
    • Assigned to - sales representative assigned to validate the lead
    • Contact information - added to a new contact record when a sales lead is validated
    • Company profile information - added to a new account record when a sales lead is validated
    • Comments - journal field entries are converted to activities on new contact and account records when a sales lead is validated


2.1 Leads and Existing Contacts

When a lead is created, if the e-mail address matches an existing contact:

  • The lead is automatically linked to the matching contact and account records.
  • The lead status is automatically set to Converted.

3 Converting Valid Sales Leads

Converted lead
To convert a valid sales lead:
  1. Open the lead record.
  2. Select Active as the Lead status.
  3. Click the Convert link.
    • A new contact is created from the contact information, and the contact record is linked to the lead record.
    • A new account is created from the company information, and the account record is linked to the lead record.
    • The lead status is changed to Converted, and the lead no longer appears in the My Leads list for the assigned representative.
    • Entries in the Comments field are converted to activities on the contact and account records.


4 Assigning Leads Automatically

Lead assignment rules automatically assign leads to sales staff for validation. For example, leads may be assigned by location or industry. Lead assignment rules work similarly to task assignment rules. They are applied when a lead is created or updated and the Assigned to field is blank. Administrators can manage lead assignment rules.

Lead Assignment Rule
To manage lead assignment rules:
  1. Navigate to Sales > Lead Assignment Rules.
  2. Click New.
  3. Enter the rule details and save the record.
    • Name
    • Description
    • User - sales person to assign for leads meeting conditions
    • Active - select to enable or clear to disable the rule
    • Execution Order - rules are tested in ascending order, and the first rule for which conditions are met is applied
    • Table - always Lead (sales_lead)
    • Conditions - if conditions are met, the lead is assigned to the specified user
