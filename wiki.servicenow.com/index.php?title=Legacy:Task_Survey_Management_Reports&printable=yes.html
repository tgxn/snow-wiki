1 Overview

Best Practice - Task Survey Management provides reports for tracking survey responses. Use the available gauges to display these reports on homepages. View survey reports on the survey overview homepage by navigating to the appropriate location for your version:

  • Eureka release or later: Survey Management > Legacy Surveys > Overview
  • Dublin release or earlier: Survey > Overview

To learn more about reports, see Report Types.

Note: A significantly improved and updated feature named Survey Management is available starting with the Eureka release. Survey components from previous versions are available in legacy modules and can be migrated.


2 Report Summary

2.1 Task Survey States 90 Days

This report displays the ratio of surveys requested versus completed over the past 90 days.


2.2 Service Desk Technician Competency 90 Day Average

This report displays the average rating of the technical competency question by task assigned to a user.



2.3 Reporting on Survey Responses

The following reports summarize responses this month for each question in the standard Service Desk Satisfaction Survey.

2.3.1 Service Desk Survey Timely Response 30 Days

Question 1: How satisfied were you with the response time to your incident?


2.3.2 Service Desk Survey Tech Courteous 30 Days

Question 2: How courteous and respectful was the technician who responded?


2.3.3 Service Desk Survey Tech Competence 30 Days

Question 3: Please rate the technical competency of the technician serving you.


2.3.4 Service Desk Survey First Call Resolve 30 Days

Question 4: Was technician able to resolve your issue during the first call?


2.3.5 Service Desk Survey Overall Experience 30 Days

Question 5: How satisfied are you with your overall service experience?
