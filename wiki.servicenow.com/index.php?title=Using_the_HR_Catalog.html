Using the HR Catalog

1 Overview

As an end user, you can use the service catalog to submit requests to HR for a variety of reasons. For example, you can submit a request to ask about the organization's maternity leave policy.

You can also view HR requests to track the progress of your cases.

2 Submitting a Case to HR

The HR catalog allows you to submit HR cases.

  1. Navigate to Self-Service > HR Catalog.
  2. Select the appropriate category and item.
  3. Fill in the form with the relevant information. Questions vary depending on the item selected.
  4. Click Submit.
    The case is submitted to HR for processing.

3 Viewing Submitted Cases

Navigate to Self-Service > My Requests to view a list of active HR cases you have submitted as requests.
