1 Overview

When the form personalization feature is activated, users can personalize fields to appear on a specific form view according to individual preferences. Personal form customizations do not affect what other users see on their forms.

In contrast to configuring a form, personalizing a form does not enable users to perform the following actions:

  • Change the order of fields on the form.
  • Add fields that are not configured to appear on the form.
  • Hide mandatory fields.

Form personalization is available in UI15 starting with the Fuji release.

Note: Personalizing a form in this way modifies the form for you only. To make changes to a form that are visible to all users, you must configure the form.


2 Personalizing Forms

By default, users with the itil role can personalize forms. To personalize a form:

  1. Open any record.
  2. In the form header, click the gear icon ().
    The Personalize Form menu appears and hide field icons appear on the form.


    Fields you have previously hidden are indicated by a cleared grey check box.
    Note: Only the fields, which the form is configured to display, are available in the Personalize Form menu. To add fields to the form, you must configure the form layout.
  3. Clear a check box or click the hide field icon () by a field to hide the field.
    Some check boxes are grey and selected. These check boxes indicate fields that cannot be hidden because they are required or are already hidden by UI policies or client scripts.
  4. Click outside the Personalize Form menu to save your changes.
    Note that if you navigate away from the form, the next time you personalize the form you must reset your customizations to show fields you have hidden.


To restore the default form view, personalize the form and click Reset.
