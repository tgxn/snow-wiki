1 Overview

The table on this page includes a description of each business rule included in the base ServiceNow system and a short description of what it it does. Depending on the version of ServiceNow you are using, some business rules on this list might not be present or might operate in a slightly different way. This list includes business rules installed by plugins that are installed by default, but does not include business rules that come with optional plugins.
